Understanding Heart Health

Heart health is crucial for overall well-being. Factors such as diet, exercise, stress, and genetics play a role in heart disease. Regular check-ups and being informed can reduce risks.

Diet and Heart Health

Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains can significantly benefit heart health. Limiting saturated fats, sugars, and sodium is also vital.

Importance of Exercise

Physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight, reduces the risk of several diseases including heart diseases, and can improve mental well-being.

Prediction Guide

Our prediction engine is designed using advanced machine learning techniques, trained on a vast dataset of patients' health records. Here's how it works:

Data Collection

We gather anonymized health data, including age, gender, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and various other health metrics.

Model Training

Using this data, our prediction model is trained to recognize patterns and factors leading to heart diseases.

Prediction

When you provide your health details, our model analyzes the data in real-time and predicts the likelihood of potential heart conditions.

Continuous Improvement

Our model is constantly evolving. With more data, it refines its predictions, making them more accurate over time.

Factors Considered

Factors include age, gender, blood pressure, cholesterol, smoking habits, family history, and more. Remember, predictions are based on statistical data and should not replace a doctor's advice.
